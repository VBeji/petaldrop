About The Protocol

Our mission is to drive DeFi participation to new heights by offering secure, intelligent and optimized trading solutions for individuals and institutional traders to tap effortlessly into the fragmented liquidity pools.

Robust API

We have a public library available for smooth integration into dApp and Wallet. Our corporate customers have access to a dedicated API key for superior reliability.

Advanced Charts

We integrate 100+ protocols across multiple chains on L1 and L2. Additionally, we complement open liquidity with exclusive pricing from the best market makers.

Multichain

Our protocol is available on multiple blockchains. We currently support Ethereum Mainnet, Binance Smart Chain, Avalanche, Polygon, Fantom, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base and zkEVM.

Onchain RFQ

petaldrop Pool is an on-chain Request for Quotes. We aggregate real-time quotes from KYC validated and trusted market makers, offering prices to all of our users.

Yield Optimizer

We have built a solution to optimize the lending rates on lending protocols.

Secured & Audited

Introducing petaldrop Delta

Revolutionizing token swaps with MEV protection and zero gas costs

Elevate Your Trading with petaldrop Delta

petaldrop Delta introduces a new way of trading on petaldrop, with improved execution, minimized MEV risks and gas costs abstraction.

By leveraging the advanced Portikus Infrastructure, petaldrop Delta delegates your swap execution to a network of settlement Agents which compete to deliver the best possible execution, all while enabling more and more trade functions to be added over time.

Unlock a smooth swap experience with petaldrop Delta best execution methods and trade tokens without worrying about gas costs or MEV attacks!
